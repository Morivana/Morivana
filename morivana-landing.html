GOT
QUESTIONS?

Does Morivana taste bitter?

No — it has a refreshing citrus flavor with natural sweetness from monk fruit. Nothing like the grassy green drinks you might have tried before.

Is it vegan?

Yes, Morivana is completely vegan and soy-free. Clean, plant-based ingredients only.

Does it contain added sugar?

No added sugar whatsoever. It uses monk fruit as a natural, zero-calorie sweetener.

Can I drink it daily?

Absolutely. It is designed and formulated for everyday use — 1 scoop in the morning is all it takes.

What makes moringa special?

Moringa is called the "Tree of Life" because it contains vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and plant nutrients in exceptionally high amounts.

Can beginners drink it?

Absolutely. It's made to be an easy, enjoyable introduction to super greens — no blending, no prep, no hassle.
