Eight Plants. Nothing Else.

Every ingredient in our super greens blend is a whole plant cold-dried, ground, and weighed. No synthesized ingredients, no proprietary extracts.

  • Moringa (Moringa oleifera): Sourced from Tamil Nadu, India. The most nutrient-dense leaf on earth with iron, calcium, and 92 antioxidants in a single scoop.
  • Spirulina (Arthrospira platensis): Pond-grown blue-green algae packed with complete protein and natural energy.
  • Amla (Phyllanthus emblica): Sourced from Uttarakhand, India. One of the richest natural sources of vitamin C on the planet.
  • Ginger (Zingiber officinale): Sourced from Kerala, India. Calms digestion and inflammation.
  • Lemon (Citrus limon): Cold-dried lemon zest for an alkalizing, brightening lift.
  • Inulin (Cichorium intybus): Soluble fiber from chicory root that feeds good gut bacteria.
  • Orange Peel (Citrus sinensis): Flavonoids and digestive enzymes from dried whole orange peel.
  • Monk Fruit (Siraitia grosvenorii): Sourced from Guangxi, China. Natural Mogroside V sweetness with zero calories.
